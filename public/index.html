How do I know this is a legit site?

All of the source code for this website is available on GitHub. I've also done my best to document the code and put descriptions of how data is used on this website.

How do I use this website?

Click the Signup / Login button here or at the top of the page, and follow the instructions to link your Google and Gradescope accounts.

How do I remove this website's access to my accounts?

On your Account Dashboard, click the button labeled Delete My Account (or just click this link), and you will be redirected to a page that will remove your account and all associated information from this website. You will also be given instructions on how to de-authorize this website from your Google and Gradescope accounts. How are you making money/paying for this site?

I am making great use of Firebase's free usage limits. The remaining usage rates are low enough that I've set a $1 price cap on the remaining usage (so please don't DDoS me). If this site becomes popular, I will probably add a donation button to cover the hosting costs. Ultimately, I plan to use this site myself, and I heard that others might be interested in the same service without the hassle of the standalone version. For the forseeable future, this will remain a free service. If it exceeds the usage cap, then it will be shut down until the next month. Conversely, if this service goes mostly unused, I may decide to shut it down and/or stop maintaining it.
